About the Game

         Duels of Dreams is a combat driven tabletop rpg system. Emphasis is on choices over randomness, realistic wounds, historically inspired and uniquely useful weapons, point buy character creation, and custom flexible magic users.

Game rules are currently in Alpha game testing, and are available for free online. Playtests occur regularly  online via Roll20 in the discord server, or locally.

Duels of Dreams is abbreviated as ‘DODAS’ (Duels of Dreams Adventure System).

Website is under development.
